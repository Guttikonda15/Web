GITAM University


Institute of Technology and Management (GITAM) is a private deemed university located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.[1] Founded by the late Dr M. V. V. S. Murthi[2] in 1980[3][4] in Visakhapatnam, it also has campus in Hyderabad and Bengaluru.[5] It was previously affiliated with Andhra University under the name GITAM College, then gained autonomy status in the year 2007.[1] GITAM is the first private engineering institute in the state to receive the University status.
